Bio

Mandy Chick is a dedicated senior at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in Terre Haute, Indiana, pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering, complemented by Minors in Economics and Entrepreneurial Studies. In addition, she is simultaneously working towards a Master's Degree in Engineering Management through R Squared.

Mandy has gained valuable experience through three engineering internships, starting as a Production Engineering Intern at Toyota Material Handling and later serving as a Design Engineer Intern at General Motors. She has recently accepted a full-time position as a Design Engineer with General Motors and will be relocating to Michigan in January.

As an entrepreneur, Mandy is passionate about growing her small business, Victory 23 Designs, which currently focuses on 3D printing. She aims to expand into various additive manufacturing techniques, laser cutting, fabrication, and custom design projects. Looking ahead, Mandy plans to pursue her Juris Doctorate degree next August, with the goal of becoming an intellectual property attorney, blending her engineering expertise with her legal aspirations.
